Founders

QBICON was founded by researchers who build the tools the field needs.

  • Founder | Director

    Subhajyoti Chaudhuri

    Theoretical & Computational Chemist, Northwestern University

    Theoretical and computational chemist and physicist. First at Yale, and now at Northwestern, Subha is developing state-of-the art theoretical and computational methods to study charge, spin, and energy transfer in complex systems. Subha's research is at the intersection of electronic structure theory, statistical physics, spin dynamics, graph theory, and machine learning.

    Website

  • Founder | Director

    Songi Han

    Mark & Nancy Ratner Professor of Chemistry, Northwestern University

    Physical chemist and magnetic-resonance spectroscopist. Songi pioneers dynamic nuclear polarization and quantum control of electron and nuclear spins to make the hidden chemistry of life visible - designing electron–nuclear spin clusters for quantum resonance sensing of molecular structure, dynamics, and assembly.

    Han Lab Profile

Building a founding scientific advisory board. If your lab works at the quantum–life interface, we want to hear from you.
