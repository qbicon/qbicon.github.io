About the workshop

Two days, one frontier

Each edition of QBi² takes a single, sharply defined topic at the intersection of quantum physics and the life sciences and gives it two days of concentrated attention — talks, working sessions, and the kind of corridor conversations that move a young field forward.

The 2026 workshop centres on protein spin qubits and their optical control inside living systems: how engineered and natural spin centres in proteins can be initialised, read out, and coherently controlled with light — and what that unlocks for sensing, imaging, and computation in biology.

Program

What to expect

  • Day 1 · Oct 19

    Foundations & frontiers

    Protein spin qubits, optical initialisation and readout, and the physics of coherence in biological environments.

  • Day 2 · Oct 20

    Control in living systems

    Optical control, in-cell sensing and imaging, and the path from proof-of-principle to application.
