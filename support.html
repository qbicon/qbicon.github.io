Support QBICON

Fund the future of quantum biology

QBICON is building open, foundational science at the edge of physics and life. Philanthropic and corporate support lets us run experiments, share data, host QBi², and train the next generation of quantum biologists — in the open, for everyone.

Why give

Your support, multiplied

  • Open by default

    Every result, method, and dataset you fund is released publicly — your support compounds across the whole field.

  • Foundational research

    We back the hard, early questions that single grants rarely cover but that the field can't advance without.

  • People & training

    Fellowships, workshops, and QBi² grow the researchers who will define quantum biology for decades.

Where it goes

What funding makes possible

  • Experiments & instruments

    Shared measurement campaigns and the specialised kit they require.

  • QBi² & convenings

    Travel grants, venues, and the gatherings that knit the community together.

  • Open data & software

    Infrastructure to publish, host, and standardise results so anyone can build on them.

  • Fellowships

    Support for students and early-career scientists at the quantum–life interface.

Ways to partner

Suggested levels — we'll tailor any partnership to your goals.

  • Supporter

    Up to $10k

    For individuals and small donors who want to back open science.

    • Named thanks on qbicon.org
    • Project updates
    • Invitation to QBi²

  • Partner

    $10k – $50k

    For organisations partnering on the mission.

    • Logo on site & QBi² materials
    • Two QBi² passes
    • Annual briefing with the founders

  • Benefactor

    $50k+

    For major backers shaping the field's foundations.

    • Premier recognition across QBICON
    • QBi² sponsorship & speaking slot
    • Named fellowship or research theme
